Cards · gopay-js-sdk

sdk.getCardDetails(cardId)

GET /cards/tokens/{card_id} — retrieve details of a stored permanent card token (masked PAN, expiry, scheme, fingerprint). Requires card:read scope.

—

sdk.tokenizeEncryptedCard(payload)

POST /cards/tokens — exchange the JWE payload produced by the card form (submitMode: 'return-payload') for a permanent card token. Call this server-side; the payload is auto-filled when the card form completes. Requires card:write scope.

—

sdk.deleteCard(cardId)

DELETE /cards/tokens/{card_id} — permanently remove a stored card token. Returns no content on success.
